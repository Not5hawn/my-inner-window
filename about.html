About My Inner Window

Helping you see yourself more clearly, one test at a time.

Our Mission

My Inner Window was created with a simple belief: understanding yourself is the first step toward a better life. We provide free, accessible personality assessments that draw on well-established psychological frameworks to help you explore who you are at your core.

Whether you're navigating a career change, improving your relationships, or simply curious about what makes you tick, our tests offer a starting point for meaningful self-reflection.

What We Offer

  • Free personality tests based on established psychological models
  • Detailed type descriptions covering strengths, growth areas, and more
  • Optional premium insights for deeper understanding
  • Shareable results so you can compare types with friends
  • New tests and content added regularly

About Our Tests

Our flagship test is based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) framework, one of the most widely used personality assessment tools in the world. The test measures preferences across four dimensions: Extraversion/Introversion, Sensing/Intuition, Thinking/Feeling, and Judging/Perceiving.

While no personality test can capture the full complexity of who you are, the MBTI framework offers valuable insights into your natural tendencies, communication style, and areas for growth.

We're committed to expanding our offerings with additional assessments including the Enneagram, Big Five (OCEAN), and more.
