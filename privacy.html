Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

The short version: Your quiz answers never leave your device. We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic, and we collect your email only if you choose to subscribe. We do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

My Inner Window ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at myinnerwindow.com. We provide free personality tests based on established psychological frameworks. You can contact us at hello@myinnerwindow.com.

2. Information We Collect

Quiz Answers & Results

Your quiz answers are processed entirely within your browser. They are never transmitted to our servers. Results are stored in your browser's sessionStorage (cleared when you close your tab) and optionally in localStorage (retained between sessions on the same device) so you can revisit them. No quiz data ever leaves your device.

Account Information

If you create an account, your name, email address, and a hashed version of your password are stored in your browser's localStorage. This information exists only on your device and is not stored on any server.

Email Address

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is collected and stored. We use it only to send you personality insights, updates, and new test announcements. We will never sell or share your email with third parties.

Usage & Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. This service automatically collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level, derived from IP address)
  • Interactions such as button clicks and form submissions

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We use it to improve the site experience. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
